Welcome to the Daily Art Progress Tracker!

Our mission - To create a website that motivate artists to keep creating!

When we first began this project, we had one thing in mind, stoping artistic burnout. It's easy to loose motivation as an artist, and we hope that by creating a place where artists can track their progress over time, artists will be able to look back on their work and be inspired by it.
